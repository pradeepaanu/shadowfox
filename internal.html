About MS Dhoni

MS Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, is a former Indian cricketer and captain who led India to numerous victories. He is known for his calm demeanor, finishing skills, and leadership on the field.

Dhoni's journey from a small-town boy to one of the most successful captains in cricket history is truly inspiring. His ability to remain calm under pressure and his strategic thinking have earned him the nickname "Captain Cool."

He has a unique batting style, with an aggressive approach in limited-overs cricket. His helicopter shot is iconic and has been emulated by many. Beyond cricket, Dhoni has also served as a Lieutenant Colonel in the Indian Territorial Army, showing his dedication to his country.

Career

Dhoni made his international debut in 2004. He became the captain of the Indian team in 2007 and led India to win the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, and the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.

Achievements

  • 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup - Winner
